Here are high-impact, practical enhancements to consider next:

Customer and Sales
*Loyalty tiers and rewards: auto-assign tiers, points, coupons
*Quotes and pro-forma invoices: convert to sales with one click
*Returns/Refunds workflow: RMA numbers, reasons, restocking impacts
*Email/SMS notifications: order status, warranty reminders, installments due

Inventory and Purchasing
*Supplier POs and receiving: generate POs, receive stock, cost updates
*Stocktakes and adjustments: cycle counts, variance reports
*Bundle/kits support: sell kits; auto-deduct components
*Barcode/QR support: scan to add to cart, receive, or lookup

Installments and Finance
*Multiple payment schedules: weekly/biweekly/custom
*Payment ledger: partial payments, receipts, export to CSV/PDF
*Automated dunning: escalating reminders for overdue accounts
*Finance reports: aging, default rates, MRR for installments

Repairs and Service
*Technician assignments and SLAs: due dates, turnaround tracking
*Parts usage: link used parts to stock deduction and costs
*Customer approvals: send estimate, digital approval, log signature
*Repair templates: common issues with predefined tasks/parts

Analytics and Reporting
*Sales dashboards by brand/model/user: time-series, cohort, funnels
*Profitability by SKU/customer: COGS roll-ups; ABC analysis
*Forecasts: demand and stock-out predictions using simple models
*Export center: scheduled CSV/PDF exports to email or S3

User Management and Security
*Role-permission matrix: granular actions vs areas
*Audit log search and export: filters, CSV/PDF
*Two-factor authentication: TOTP (Authy/Google Authenticator)
*Session control: force logout, device/session list

UX and Productivity
*Global search command bar: jump to laptops, customers, repairs
*Keyboard shortcuts: cart operations, quick-add, save
*Inline editing in tables: reduce modal usage
*Dark/light auto theming per system time

Backups and Integrations
*Offsite backups: S3/Azure/Google Cloud with retention policy
*Versioned backups: diff/restore a specific table only
*Accounting integration: QuickBooks/Xero sync (customers, invoices, payments)
*Email providers: switch mock to Resend/SendGrid with templates

E-commerce and Omnichannel
*Public catalog (optional): shareable product pages; reserve in-store
*Payment gateways: Stripe/Adyen for card payments
*Tax/VAT handling: per-region rules, invoices with tax breakdowns
*Shipping integrations: label printing, tracking links

Developer and Ops
*Migrations and seed suites: dev/demo/production seed profiles
*Background jobs: queue for emails, reports, backups (BullMQ/Cloud Tasks)
*Observability: Sentry for errors, OpenTelemetry traces, audit dashboards
*Test coverage: unit (Jest), component (RTL), e2e (Playwright/Cypress)

Data Hygiene and Quality
*Validation rules: duplicate detection for customers and products
*Enrichment: auto-fetch laptop specs by model (optional API)
*GDPR tooling: export/delete customer data on request
